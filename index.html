Profile

I am a Software Engineer with a passion for iOS development and all things iOS. In the summer of 2016, I worked at a startup in San Francisco, CA, called PubNub. They are a global data stream network that provide API's for developers to use. I built two small iOS apps and a tvOS app in Swift using their API. I then moved to the client engineering team to work alongside a iOS developer to help build a Cocoapod for a PubNub debug console to allow developers to add to the apps they're building.

I'm currently working on a fashion iOS app alongside my two friends and team members I've been doing outside projects with for a few years now. I hold the role as lead iOS developer in this project. Besides iOS, I've built several small projects in languages such as scala, lisp, python, prolog and I've built many projects in Java. I programmed, large, extensive projects in Java for most of my Computer Science courses, courses like: Parallel Computing, Networking, Graphical User Interfaces and Data Structures and File Processing to name a few.

Besides programming, I love learning about anything tech related, have a passion for the sport of boxing, playing the guitar and will always enjoy a hike to the top of a high peak in the Adirondacks.

At the end of the day, my ultimate goal is to impact lives for the better with what I build.
